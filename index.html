Advanced Subtitle Editor

Aegisub is a free, cross-platform open source tool for creating and modifying subtitles. Designed for translation, karaoke, and typography.

Download Aegisub Learn More

Why Aegisub?

🎵

Visual Audio Sync

Time subtitles to audio precisely with the advanced audio spectrum and waveform view.

🎨

Full ASS Support

Native support for the advanced SubStation Alpha format. Complex typography and positioning made easy.

⚙️

Automation

Write your own macros and karaoke effects using the Lua-based Automation engine.

🎬

Real-time Video

See your typesetting in action immediately with live video preview.
